Get Started Prototype

The customer journey from application submission to first drawdown, and beyond.
* Ignore specific copy throughout. Focus on flows, resolution patterns, and structure.

Why this matters

Every customer type — eCom, wholesale, retail/services — will flow through this experience to resolve data validation issues. The task primitives (connect, upload, text response) are designed to cover every AIR and DQ scenario. Where we can't yet fully automate resolution (e.g. some staff AIRs), we still surface the requirements in-app, navigate the customer to the right place, and give them a clear path to support. This lets us coach customers through while we build out the backend to handle it end-to-end. The offer tool and rep workflows sit alongside this but aren't covered here.

What we're changing

1.All task resolution happens in the side panel. Every AIR, DQ, and post-onboarding task is resolvable without leaving the current page. Three primitives: connect, upload, text response.
2.KYC populates Company Details. Account setup flows into the Company Details tab. Once complete, fields lock; changes require support as they may trigger re-review.
3.Connections & Data shows upload status. File uploads live under their category (banking, sales, etc.) with validity and expiry indicators. No more files disappearing into the abyss.
4.Get Started becomes Overview post-funding. After first drawdown, the nav restructures to Overview / Banking / Financing / Connections & Data. Tasks move to a top-right icon, still resolved via side panels.
5.Banking upsell opportunity. The Banking tab prompts KYC completion as a gateway to a Wayflyer bank account, giving us always-fresh data.
6.Referrals visible throughout. Earn $1,000 per referral via a shareable link, accessible from the nav at all times.

Task resolution types

Every task resolves to one of three actions: connect a data source, upload a file, or respond via text. Use the "Task panel" dropdown in the scenario bar to demo each type.

Customer App AIR — Connect
Fully built in-app resolution in the side panel. These already exist on the review page today; we're moving them into the side panel for consistency. The customer resolves everything without leaving Get Started.
Staff AIR — Connect / Upload
Previously email-only. Now the email template content lives in-app: we spell out the connection requirements, link to Connections & Data, and provide a support fallback. Goal: migrate these toward full Customer App AIRs over time.
KYC AIR — Upload
Document mismatch identified during review. Customer uploads a corrected file directly in the side panel.
Underwriting DQ — Text response
Specific question from the review team. Customer types a response and optionally attaches a supporting document.
